### 2. Grammar Patterns

This week we practiced **simple weather expressions** and **location phrases**. 

Russian often uses short, impersonal sentences for weather:
- **Холодно.** = It’s cold.
- **На улице тепло.** = It’s warm outside.
- **Идёт дождь.** = Rain is falling / It’s raining.

We also saw the preposition **в** (in) + **лесу** (forest — special form of лес):
- **В лесу живёт медведь.** = A bear lives in the forest.

These patterns let you describe the world around you with very few words — perfect for beginners!

---

### 3. Culture Corner Recap

We talked about how Russians love discussing **погода** because the weather in Russia can change dramatically (from bright **солнце** to sudden **гроза**). We also explored how **медведь** (bear) is a beloved symbol in Russian culture — appearing in fairy tales, as a national mascot, and even in jokes. The **лес** (forest) holds a special place in the Russian imagination as a place of mystery, magic, and wildlife.
