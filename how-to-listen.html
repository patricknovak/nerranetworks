How to listen

Every Nerra Network show is available on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and as a standard RSS feed. Pick your app and subscribe — new episodes show up automatically.

🎧

Apple Podcasts

  1. Open the Podcasts app (iOS / Mac).
  2. Tap Search, type a show name (e.g. "Tesla Shorts Time").
  3. Tap Follow — or use the direct link below.
🟢

Spotify

  1. Open Spotify (mobile, desktop, or web).
  2. Search for the show name.
  3. Tap Follow.
📡

RSS (any app)

  1. Copy the show's RSS URL from the table below.
  2. In your podcast app, choose Add by URL / Subscribe by URL.
  3. Paste the URL and save.

Common questions

Do I need an account to listen?

No. All Nerra Network shows are free and public — no sign-up, no paywall. An Apple or Spotify account helps you save shows to your library and sync across devices, but every episode is also available via RSS.

Can I download episodes for offline listening?

Yes. Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Overcast, Pocket Casts — every major podcast app supports offline download. Look for a download icon next to each episode.

How often are new episodes released?

Most shows publish daily; a few alternate even/odd days. Each show page lists its exact schedule.

Are there ads?

No host-read ads, no programmatic mid-rolls. Nerra Network is independent and ad-free.

How do I support the shows?

Subscribe on Apple or Spotify, leave a review (those really help discoverability), share an episode you liked with a friend, and join the newsletter for weekly highlights.
