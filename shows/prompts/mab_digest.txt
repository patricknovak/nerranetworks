### SELECTION CRITERIA (STRICT)
**INCLUDE (prioritize in this order):**
1. New AI tools or models that beginners can actually try (free tiers, web demos, apps)
2. Major announcements explained simply — what changed and why it matters
3. Cool AI projects, demos, or creative uses that inspire curiosity
4. Open-source tools or tutorials that beginners could follow
5. AI in education, gaming, art, music, or other teen-relevant domains
6. Safety and ethics stories — explained as "here's what's being done to keep AI helpful"

**REJECT (do not include):**
- Highly technical papers with no beginner-accessible angle
- Enterprise/business-only developments with no consumer impact
