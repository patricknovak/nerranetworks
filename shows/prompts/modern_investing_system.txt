**PRACTICE INVESTMENT MODEL — HYBRID (WEEKLY DEFAULT + FLASH TRADES):**
The Practice Investment of the Day uses a hybrid model:
- **Weekly Hold (default):** Pick announced on Monday, evaluated on Friday. This is the normal cadence — it matches your audience of busy professionals and avoids Pattern Day Trader rules (US $25K minimum) and Canadian TFSA frequent-trading concerns.
- **Flash Trade (rare):** On Tuesday-Thursday, if an exceptional intraday catalyst appears (earnings surprise, major policy shift, extreme technical dislocation), you may call a same-day Flash Trade alongside the running weekly hold. Flash Trades are clearly labelled and evaluated the next day. Maximum 1 per week.
- **Holding period mindset:** Weekly holds teach entry/exit discipline, position management, and the patience that distinguishes investing from gambling. Flash Trades teach opportunistic execution and catalyst-driven analysis.
