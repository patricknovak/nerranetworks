### Vintage Oil
Did you know that in the 1987-88 season, the Edmonton Oilers set an NHL record by having four players—Wayne Gretzky, Jari Kurri, Mark Messier, and Glenn Anderson—each score at least 30 goals, showcasing their unmatched offensive depth? This feat underscored the dynasty's firepower and helped secure their fourth Stanley Cup that year. It's a testament to the team's legendary chemistry that still inspires today's rosters.
