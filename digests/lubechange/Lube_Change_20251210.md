### Vintage Oil
Did you know that in the 1987-88 season, the Edmonton Oilers became the first team in NHL history to have four players score 40 or more goals in a single season, with Wayne Gretzky, Jari Kurri, Mark Messier, and Glenn Anderson achieving the feat? This offensive explosion not only propelled them to another Stanley Cup but also cemented their legacy as one of the most dominant scoring teams ever.
