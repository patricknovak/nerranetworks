### Top 15 Science & Health Discoveries
1. **New moonquake discovery could change NASA’s Moon plans: 07 December, 2025, 12:15 AM PST, Science Daily**  
   Scientists have pinpointed moonquakes, rather than meteoroids, as the cause of shifting terrain near the Apollo 17 site, revealing an active fault that's still generating seismic activity. This breakthrough matters for human longevity and exploration by enhancing safety protocols for future lunar missions, ensuring astronauts can thrive in sustainable habitats while minimizing risks from unpredictable lunar geology. It also promotes planetary consciousness by fostering innovations in space technology that respect and adapt to celestial environments, paving the way for compassionate, earth-inspired stewardship beyond our world. Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2025/12/251205054743.htm

2. **The rotten egg smell that could finally beat nail fungus: 06 December, 2025, 11:03 PM PST, Science Daily**  
   Researchers have harnessed hydrogen sulfide, known for its rotten egg odor, as a potent treatment for stubborn nail infections, penetrating nails more effectively than existing drugs and eliminating pathogens. This discovery advances human health by offering a natural, efficient solution to common fungal issues that affect mobility and quality of life, potentially extending active years through better preventive care. By drawing from nature's own compounds, it aligns with sustainable innovation, reducing reliance on synthetic chemicals and promoting eco-friendly health practices that honor the planet's resources. Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2025/12/251205054741.htm

3. **Natural hormone unlocks a hidden fat burning switch: 06 December, 2025, 07:32 AM PST, Science Daily**  
   The hormone FGF19 has been found to activate the brain's energy-burning mechanisms and fat-burning cells, presenting a promising avenue for obesity treatments that enhance thermogenesis and curb inflammation. This matters for longevity by addressing metabolic health at its core, helping individuals achieve sustained vitality and resilience against age-related diseases. Embracing such natural biological pathways encourages sustainable wellness innovations that integrate compassion for human bodies with environmental harmony, minimizing the ecological footprint of pharmaceutical interventions. Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2025/12/251205054739.htm

4. **AI’s climate impact is much smaller than many feared: 06 December, 2025, 06:33 AM PST, Science Daily**  
   A new analysis of U.S. economic data reveals that AI's energy consumption is far lower than anticipated, challenging fears of it being a major environmental threat and highlighting its efficient integration across industries. This finding boosts planetary well-being by validating AI as a tool for sustainable progress, enabling innovations in health and longevity without excessive carbon costs. It inspires forward-thinking approaches where technology serves as a compassionate ally in combating climate challenges, fostering a healthier Earth for future generations. Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2025/12/251205054736.htm

5. **Scientists find hidden layers in brain’s memory center: 06 December, 2025, 05:07 AM PST, Science Daily**  
   Using advanced RNA imaging, researchers have revealed a four-layer structure in the hippocampal CA1 region, a key area for memory, navigation, and emotion, uncovering previously hidden complexities in brain architecture. This breakthrough enhances human health and longevity by deepening our understanding of cognitive functions, potentially leading to targeted therapies for memory-related disorders and improved mental resilience. By advancing neuroscience through precise, low-impact technologies, it supports sustainable innovation that respects the intricate balance of life, blending compassion for human minds with stewardship of our planet's intellectual frontiers.
