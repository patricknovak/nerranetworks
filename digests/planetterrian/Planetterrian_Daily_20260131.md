### Top 15 Science & Health Discoveries
1. **Babies share microbiomes at nursery: 30 January 2026 • Nature Medicine**  
   A study revealed that infants in daycare exchange gut bacteria through social interactions, influencing microbiome development. This could enhance early immune health and longevity by fostering diverse microbial ecosystems.  
   Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/d41591-026-00006-6

2. **Positive thinking strengthens vaccine immunity: 30 January 2026 • Science News**  
   Research found that optimistic mindsets boosted brain activity and antibody responses post-vaccination. Such mental strategies may improve overall immune function, supporting longer, healthier lives.  
   Source: https://www.sciencenews.org/article/placebo-brain-activity-vaccine-antibody

3. **Alzheimer's brain damage without symptoms explained: 30 January 2026 • New Scientist**

5. **No autism link to paracetamol confirmed: 30 January 2026 • Nature Medicine**  
   Advanced genetic analysis debunked associations between prenatal paracetamol use and autism risk. This clarifies safety for expectant parents, aiding informed choices for child health and development.

7. **Genes influence longevity more than expected: 29 January 2026 • Science News**
