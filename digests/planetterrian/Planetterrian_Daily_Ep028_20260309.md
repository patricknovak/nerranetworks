### Top 15 Science & Health Discoveries
1. **Particles may deviate from Einstein’s geodesics** : 09 March 2026 • Science Daily  
   Researchers at TU Wien developed a quantum version of geodesic paths, showing particles in quantum spacetime might not follow classical relativity predictions. This advances efforts to unify quantum mechanics and gravity, potentially reshaping our understanding of particle behavior in the universe.  
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/03/260308201613.htm

2. **Magnets engineered to mimic graphene’s properties** : 09 March 2026 • Science Daily  
   Engineers created a magnetic film with hexagonal holes, enabling spin waves to behave like electrons in graphene. This connection between electronic and magnetic systems opens new ways to study and design complex magnetic materials for technology.  
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/03/260307213230.htm

3. **Parental stress linked to childhood obesity** : 09 March 2026 • Science Daily  
   A Yale study showed that mindfulness and stress management for parents led to healthier eating and less weight gain in children compared to diet-focused interventions. Reducing parental stress could be a key strategy in preventing early obesity and promoting family-wide health benefits.  
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/03/260307213228.htm

4. **Microbiome modulation boosts cancer immunotherapy** : 09 March 2026 • Nature  
   Three trials demonstrated that fecal microbiota transplantation improves immunotherapy outcomes in advanced solid tumors, offering insights for microbiome-based therapies. This could expand effective treatments for resistant cancers and guide future drug development.  
   Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-026-04285-x

5. **Vaccines advance against mosquito-borne viruses** : 09 March 2026 • Nature  
   New vaccines and tools target diseases like chikungunya, dengue, yellow fever, and Zika amid rising threats from urbanization and climate change. These innovations aim to curb outbreaks and protect vulnerable populations globally.  
   Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/d41591-026-00014-6

6. **Ketamine reshapes brain activity for depression relief** : 08 March 2026 • Science Daily  
   Brain scans revealed ketamine alters a key receptor in mood-related regions, correlating with rapid symptom improvement in treatment-resistant depression. This could refine predictions of patient response and inspire targeted therapies.  
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/03/260307213234.htm

7. **Risperidone raises stroke risk in dementia patients** : 08 March 2026 • Science Daily  
   A UK study of 165,000 patients found risperidone increases stroke risk even in those without prior heart issues, questioning its safety for agitation management. Clinicians may need to explore alternatives for dementia care.  
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/03/260307213244.htm

8. **Increasing fiber intake transforms gut health** : 08 March 2026 • Science Daily  
   Researchers highlighted how dietary fiber supports digestion, gut microbes, blood sugar control, and reduces risks of obesity, diabetes, and cancers. Adopting higher fiber habits could lead to widespread preventive health gains.  
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/03/260307213252.htm

This approach could help patients with hard-to-treat cancers like melanoma or lung cancer, especially those who don't respond to standard therapies, by harnessing the gut's role in immune function.
