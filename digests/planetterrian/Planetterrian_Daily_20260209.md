### Top 15 Science & Health Discoveries
1. **Sensitive Cancer Antigen Detection via Peptide Libraries: 09 February 2026 • Nature Biotechnology**  
   Researchers developed user-defined peptide libraries for highly sensitive detection of cancer antigens, enhancing insights into regulatory T cell biology. This advancement could improve early cancer diagnosis, boosting longevity by enabling timely interventions without forcing unsustainable resource use.  
   Source: [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41587-026-03003-9]

2. **Rate Variation Challenges in Pandemic-Scale Phylogenetics: 09 February 2026 • Nature Methods**  
   A study revealed how rate variation and recurrent sequence errors complicate large-scale phylogenetic analysis during pandemics. Understanding these factors strengthens global health responses, promoting longer lives through better disease tracking and eco-friendly data-driven prevention strategies.  
   Source: [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-025-02932-8]

3. **Deep Single-Cell Metabolomics with Ion Mobility Mass Cytometry: 09 February 2026 • Nature Methods**

   Source: [https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-025-02970-2]

6. **Xenogeneic Pig Livers Support Human Liver Function Extracorporeally: 09 February 2026 • Nature Medicine**
