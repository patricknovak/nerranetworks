### Top 15 Science & Health Discoveries
1. **Toxic Metals in Bananas Post-Disaster: 17 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   Researchers found toxic metals like lead and cadmium in bananas, cassava, and cocoa after Brazil’s 2015 mining disaster. This raises concerns for children’s health with long-term exposure risks, while highlighting the need for sustainable land recovery to protect food systems.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260217005756.htm

2. **Ancient DNA Reveals Growth Disorder: 17 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   DNA from a 12,000-year-old Italian burial confirmed a rare genetic growth disorder in a mother and daughter. This discovery deepens our understanding of prehistoric health challenges and genetic continuity in human history.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260217005754.htm

3. **Brain Receptors Clear Alzheimer’s Plaques: 17 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   Scientists identified brain receptors that boost amyloid-breaking enzymes, reducing Alzheimer’s plaques in mice and improving memory. This could lead to affordable, low-side-effect treatments, transforming care for aging populations.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260215225555.htm

4. **Hidden Cancer RNAs Uncovered: 17 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   A mysterious RNA in breast cancer revealed a new class of cancer-specific molecules across tumor types, aiding diagnosis via blood tests. This breakthrough enhances early detection and personalized treatment, improving survival odds.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260216084527.htm

5. **Quantum Encryption Flaw Exposed: 17 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   Researchers developed a framework showing how tiny misalignments disrupt quantum key distribution, threatening secure communication. Addressing this could safeguard health data privacy in future digital systems.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260215225608.htm

6. **Heart Health Tied to Food Quality: 17 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   A study of 200,000 adults showed heart disease risk drops with diets rich in whole grains and plant-based foods, not just low-carb or low-fat. Prioritizing sustainable food choices can protect both human and planetary health.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260216084521.htm

7. **Cash Transfers Don’t Increase Harm: 17 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   An 11-year Alaska study found no rise in injuries or deaths after cash dividend payments, debunking reckless behavior fears. This supports equitable health policies that empower communities without unintended consequences.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260212023028.htm

8. **Blood Test Detects Cancer Early: 17 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   A light-based sensor detects tiny cancer biomarkers in blood, succeeding in lung cancer patient samples. This could enable portable, early detection tools, revolutionizing how we catch and treat diseases.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260216044002.htm

9. **Antibiotic Stewardship Succeeds in China: 17 February 2026 • Nature**
   A trial in rural China reduced antibiotic prescriptions for respiratory infections through training and feedback programs. This curbs antimicrobial resistance, preserving effective treatments for future generations.
   Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-026-04222-y

10. **Growth Hormone Impact on Aging Brains: 16 February 2026 • Lifespan.io**
    Silencing a growth hormone receptor in aged male mice slowed mental aging and boosted cognitive performance. This opens pathways to combat age-related decline, potentially extending healthy lifespans.
    Source: https://lifespan.io/news/silencing-growth-hormone-has-strong-effects-in-mouse-brains/?utm_source=rss&utm_medium=rss&utm_campaign=silencing-growth-hormone-has-strong-effects-in-mouse-brains

11. **Psychedelic Eases Depression Symptoms: 16 February 2026 • New Scientist**
    A single dose of DMT, paired with therapy, reduced depression symptoms more than a placebo. This offers hope for rapid mental health interventions with lasting impact.
    Source: https://www.newscientist.com/article/2515598-psychedelic-reduces-depression-symptoms-after-just-one-dose/?utm_campaign=RSS%7CNSNS&utm_source=NSNS&utm_medium=RSS&utm_content=home

12. **Majorana Qubits Advance Quantum Computing: 16 February 2026 • Science Daily**
    Scientists decoded Majorana qubits’ hidden states, confirming their noise resistance and millisecond coherence for robust quantum computers. This could accelerate medical research through faster, secure data processing.
    Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260216084525.htm

13. **Brain Parasite Kill Switch Found: 16 February 2026 • Science Daily**
    Research revealed how immune cells self-destruct to kill Toxoplasma gondii, a common brain parasite, preventing illness. This insight could inspire new defenses against latent infections.
    Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260215225602.htm

14. **Lab-Grown Spinal Cord Heals Injury: 16 February 2026 • Science Daily**
    A human mini spinal cord model in the lab regenerated nerve fibers after injury using “dancing molecules” therapy. This could pave the way for repairing real spinal damage, restoring mobility.
    Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260216044003.htm

15. **Brain Inflammation Drives Compulsive Behavior: 16 February 2026 • Science Daily**
    Rat studies showed brain inflammation, not habit, fuels compulsive behavior via astrocyte disruption in decision-making regions. This reframes mental health treatment, targeting inflammation for better outcomes.
    Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260215225606.htm
