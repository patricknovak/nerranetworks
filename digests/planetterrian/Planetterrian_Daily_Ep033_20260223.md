### Top 15 Science & Health Discoveries
1. **Blood Test Predicts Alzheimer’s Onset: 23 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   A new blood test measuring p-tau217 protein can forecast Alzheimer’s symptoms within 3-4 years. This could revolutionize early interventions and personalize care for millions at risk.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260222085203.htm

2. **Brains Tune Out: How to Refocus: 23 February 2026 • New Scientist**
   Research reveals why our brains ignore familiar stimuli to avoid sensory overload. Learning to notice again can enhance mental clarity and emotional well-being.
   Source: https://www.newscientist.com/article/2515746-why-our-brains-tune-things-out-and-how-to-overcome-it-when-you-need-to/?utm_campaign=RSS%7CNSNS&utm_source=NSNS&utm_medium=RSS&utm_content=home

3. **Cosmic Snowmen Form in Kuiper Belt: 23 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   Simulations from Michigan State University show gravitational collapse creates two-lobed “snowmen” shapes in the Kuiper Belt. Understanding these formations deepens insights into planetary system evolution, inspiring sustainable space exploration.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260222085206.htm

4. **Mars Volcano Reveals Complex Magma History: 23 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   Orbital imaging uncovers a Martian volcano’s multi-phase eruptions driven by an evolving underground magma system. This discovery reshapes our view of Mars’ active interior, fueling future research for human exploration.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260222092329.htm

5. **Transposable Elements Shape Dark Genome: 23 February 2026 • Nature**
   Advances in studying transposable elements in the non-coding “dark genome” reveal their role in disease and potential therapeutic targets. Unlocking this could transform treatments for complex conditions.
   Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41587-026-03012-8

6. **Y Chromosome Loss Linked to Diabetes: 23 February 2026 • Nature**
   A study of 300,000 males shows Y chromosome loss impacts type 2 diabetes risk differently across populations. This insight into glucose metabolism could lead to targeted therapies.
   Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-026-04213-z

7. **AI Designs Genomes, Redefining Evolution: 23 February 2026 • Nature**
   AI tools are accelerating the creation of synthetic organisms, pushing the boundaries of biology. This could unlock medical and environmental innovations if guided by ethical, sustainable principles.
   Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-026-00566-0

8. **Pecans Boost Heart Health Significantly: 22 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   A comprehensive review confirms pecans improve cholesterol and antioxidant defenses for cardiovascular health. Adding this sustainable, native nut to diets could prevent heart disease naturally.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260221000317.htm

9. **Triceratops Nose Cooled Massive Head: 22 February 2026 • Science Daily**
   CT scans reveal Triceratops’ complex nasal system likely regulated heat in its giant head. This finding enhances our grasp of extinct species’ adaptations, informing biome-inspired health tech.
   Source: https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2026/02/260221000313.htm

10. **Howard University Achieves Top Research Status: 23 February 2026 • Nature**
    Howard University becomes the first historically Black institution to reach R1 research status. This milestone expands access to cutting-edge science, benefiting diverse communities.
    Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-026-00366-6

11. **Victorian Voyages to Vanishing Maps: 23 February 2026 • Nature**
    A review of science books highlights historical explorations and their modern relevance. These stories inspire curiosity and remind us to preserve Earth’s disappearing landscapes.
    Source: https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-026-00567-z
