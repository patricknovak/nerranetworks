### Jargon Guide
- **Chain of Thought (CoT)**: A way AI models think step-by-step to solve problems, like how you'd break down a tough homework question into smaller parts.
- **AI Model**: A smart computer program trained on tons of data to do tasks like answering questions or generating images, think of it like a super-powered autocomplete on your phone.
- **Compute Capacity**: The tech "muscle" (like powerful computers) needed to run AI smoothly, similar to how too many apps slow down your laptop.
- **Copyright**: Rules protecting who owns creative work, like not copying someone's art without permission—AI has to follow this too when using data.
- **Prototype**: An early version of an idea, like sketching a rough draft of a game before making it real, which AI can help build quickly.
- **Gemini**: Google's family of AI chatbots that can chat, create, or explain things, imagine it as a helpful robot friend in your browser.
