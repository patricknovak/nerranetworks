### Jargon Guide
- **AI Model**: A smart computer program trained on tons of data to do tasks like writing or analyzing, like a brain that's learned from reading the entire internet.
- **Open-Source**: Software that's free for anyone to use, modify, or share, imagine it as a recipe book everyone can add to without paying.
- **Gemini**: Google's family of AI models that power tools like search or chat, think of it as a super-versatile assistant handling words, images, and more.
- **Anonymous Accounts**: Secret online profiles where you don't share your real name, like a hidden diary on social media for private thoughts.
- **Transparency Tags**: Labels that clearly show if something (like music) was made with AI, similar to ingredient lists on food to help you know what's real.
- **Genome**: The complete set of DNA in living things, like a blueprint for how bodies are built, which AI can now help decode faster.
