### JARGON GUIDE
- **AI Agent**: A smart program that acts on its own to complete tasks, like a virtual assistant that doesn't just answer questions but actually does stuff for you.
- **Chain of Thought**: A way AI thinks step-by-step, like breaking down a math problem into smaller parts before solving it.
- **Multimodal Model**: An AI that handles different types of info, such as text, images, and sounds, all at once—like chatting about a photo it "sees."
- **Mixture-of-Experts**: A setup where an AI picks from specialized "mini-brains" for each job, making it faster and smarter without using extra power.
- **Reasoning**: How AI figures things out logically, similar to how you puzzle through a riddle or plan a game strategy.
