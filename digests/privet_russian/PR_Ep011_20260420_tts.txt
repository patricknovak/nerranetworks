Now let's look at a simple grammar idea that ties everything together. In Russian every noun has a gender. Think of it like belonging to a team. The team changes a little word like my.

Most nouns ending in a or ya are feminine. So we say моя школа and моя книга.

In English we just say the teacher for anyone. But Russian picks the right sticker. Мой for masculine nouns. Моя for feminine nouns.
