### Cosmic Spotlight
The transit of Europa across Jupiter, as detailed in story #5, showcases one of our solar system's most intriguing moons casting its shadow on the gas giant's swirling clouds, visible through backyard telescopes under clear skies. Astronomers track these events using precise orbital data from missions like Galileo and Juno, revealing Europa's icy surface and subsurface ocean that may harbor conditions for life. This phenomenon not only highlights the dynamic dance of Jupiter's satellites but also fuels excitement for upcoming probes like NASA's Europa Clipper, set to investigate habitability up close.
