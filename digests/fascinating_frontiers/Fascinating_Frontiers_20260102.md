### Cosmic Spotlight
The discovery of a free-floating planet's mass and distance marks a pivotal moment in exoplanet science. Using advanced telescopes, astronomers captured this rogue world's gravitational microlensing effects, determining it's about 3 Earth masses and 17 light-years away—closer than expected for such isolates. This finding suggests rogue planets may outnumber stars in our galaxy, reshaping theories on planetary ejection from forming systems. What might we learn if we could visit one of these wandering worlds?
