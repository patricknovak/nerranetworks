### Cosmic Spotlight
Astronomers have pinpointed the first strong evidence of "monster stars" in the early universe through the EIGER survey's analysis of quasar SDSS J0100+2802, revealing these colossal entities as key drivers of rapid galaxy growth billions of years ago. By examining emission lines and intergalactic gas from the epoch of reionization, researchers confirmed these stars' immense mass and luminosity, far exceeding modern stellar limits. This discovery could unlock new insights into how the universe transitioned from its dark ages to a star-filled cosmos, potentially guiding future observations with advanced telescopes.
