### Cosmic Spotlight
The Hubble Space Telescope's image of "Dracula’s Chivito" unveils a vast, chaotic disk where stars and planets form amid swirling gas and dust, captured through its advanced imaging of infrared and visible light. This enormous structure, larger than any previously observed, shows how intense radiation and winds from young stars sculpt planetary nurseries over cosmic timescales. As astronomers analyze the data, it could reveal patterns in exoplanet diversity and stability. What hidden worlds do you think might emerge from such turbulent beginnings?
