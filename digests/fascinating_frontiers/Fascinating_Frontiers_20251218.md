### Cosmic Spotlight
The James Webb Space Telescope has confirmed the existence of a supermassive black hole hurtling through the "Cosmic Owl" galaxies at an astonishing 2.2 million miles per hour, marking the first verified "runaway" of its kind. By analyzing infrared data, astronomers observed the black hole's immense gravitational forces disrupting surrounding stars and gas, a phenomenon requiring colossal energies to eject it from its original galaxy. This breakthrough could reveal how black holes influence galaxy mergers and evolution over cosmic timescales, with potential follow-up observations to track its path.
