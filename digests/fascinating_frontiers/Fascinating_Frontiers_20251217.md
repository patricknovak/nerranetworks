### Cosmic Spotlight

Scientists detected it via JWST's infrared capabilities, spotting a trail of shocked gas and newborn stars in its wake, proving the immense gravitational forces at play. This finding could reshape theories on black hole mergers and galaxy collisions, hinting at more such wanderers in the universe. What other hidden cosmic nomads might JWST reveal next?
