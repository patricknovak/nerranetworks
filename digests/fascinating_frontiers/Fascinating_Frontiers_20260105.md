### Cosmic Spotlight

By analyzing these emissions, astronomers can better measure the black hole's spin rate, confirming theories about how these cosmic giants devour matter and influence galaxy formation. This milestone from Japan's XRISM mission opens doors to studying more distant black holes, potentially uncovering hidden patterns in the universe's evolution.
