### Cosmic Spotlight
The Hubble Space Telescope's confirmation of Betelgeuse's companion star Siwarha unveils a dynamic interplay in this red supergiant system, where the companion's orbit carves a visible wake through Betelgeuse's expelled material. By combining Hubble's ultraviolet observations with ground-based data, scientists traced this interaction, revealing how binary stars influence each other's evolution and potential supernova fates. This discovery could refine predictions for Betelgeuse's dramatic end, offering a glimpse into the life cycles of massive stars across the galaxy.
