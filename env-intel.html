About Environmental Intelligence

Environmental Intelligence delivers a daily briefing on environmental regulation, compliance requirements, and environmental science relevant to British Columbia professionals.

Hosted by Patrick in Vancouver, each episode distills the latest regulatory updates, scientific findings, and policy developments into a concise, actionable briefing for consultants, regulators, and environmental practitioners.
