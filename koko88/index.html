Meet Olya

From Odessa With Love, Building Community in Vancouver

I'm Olya — born in Odessa, Ukraine, now raising my family in beautiful Vancouver, Canada. I believe that every woman has the power to create the life she dreams of, and that we're stronger when we support each other.

Koko88 Club is my way of bringing together the things I'm most passionate about: timeless style, strong family values, wellness, the incredible possibilities of AI, and most importantly — genuine human connection.

Whether you're navigating a new country, building a career, raising a family, or simply looking for your tribe — you belong here.
