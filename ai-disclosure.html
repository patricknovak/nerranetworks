AI Content Disclosure

Nerra Network • Effective: March 31, 2026

Nerra Network is transparent about its use of artificial intelligence tools in content creation. This policy explains what we use AI for, how we maintain editorial integrity, and why we believe AI-assisted content—with human direction—is beneficial for our audience.

Transparency is our strength, not our weakness

Our Commitment to Transparency

Nerra Network uses AI tools to enhance content quality and production speed, but humans maintain full editorial control. AI assists with research and synthesis; Patrick Novak (human creator) directs strategy, selects topics, fact-checks, and approves all final content.

This hybrid approach allows Nerra Network to deliver daily, high-quality content while maintaining editorial integrity and human judgment in every decision that matters.

How We Use AI

Specific AI Applications

Research Assistance

AI helps gather, organize, and synthesize information from multiple sources. Patrick reviews all research for accuracy and relevance before use.

Content Summarization

AI assists in condensing longer documents or sources into key points. All summaries are reviewed for accuracy and context.

Text-to-Speech

AI narration tools generate audio from written content. Patrick selects tone and verifies output quality before publishing.

Outline Generation

AI suggests content structure and organization. Patrick refines, rejects, or modifies these outlines as needed.

These tools make content creation faster and allow daily output, but they do not make editorial decisions.

Why This Matters: AI-Assisted is Better

We believe transparency about AI use is a competitive advantage, not a liability:

  • Honesty builds trust: We tell you how we work instead of hiding it
  • Better content: AI assistance allows daily output without sacrificing quality
  • Efficiency: AI handles routine research and synthesis, freeing Patrick to focus on editorial judgment
  • Scalability: With AI assistance, we can cover more topics and produce more content for our audience
  • Consistency: AI tools help maintain consistent quality and formatting across all content

Many content creators use AI without disclosing it. We believe the honest approach is stronger.

Regulatory Compliance

FTC Guidelines (United States)

FTC AI Disclosures

The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) issued guidance on AI-generated content disclosures. Nerra Network complies by:

  • Clearly disclosing use of AI tools in content creation
  • Being specific about which tools we use and what they do
  • Emphasizing human editorial control and final review
  • Maintaining transparency as content is created

Guidance: FTC Warns Companies About AI Disclosures

California AI Transparency Act (AB 942)

California AB 942 Compliance

California's AI Transparency Act (effective January 2026) requires disclosure of deceptive AI-generated content used for advertising or political purposes. Nerra Network is not a political entity and does not use deceptive AI for advertising, but we proactively disclose all AI use regardless.

  • This disclosure satisfies California law requirements
  • We transparently identify AI-assisted content
  • We maintain human editorial control

Copyright & Ownership

AI-Generated Content Copyright

US Copyright Office Position (March 2026): The US Copyright Office and US Supreme Court have determined that AI-generated content cannot be copyrighted without substantial human authorship.

At Nerra Network:

  • All content is substantially written or directed by Patrick (human)
  • AI is used as a tool, not as the primary author
  • Patrick's editorial direction, fact-checking, and approval constitute substantial human authorship
  • Therefore, Nerra Network content is protected by copyright
  • You may not reproduce, distribute, or commercialize our content without permission
